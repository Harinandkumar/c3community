Creative Coding Community (C3)

Recruitment Rule Book

Interview Date

5th August 2025

Objective

To ensure a transparent, fair, and merit-based recruitment process for selecting Coordinators and Sub-Coordinators who can lead and contribute effectively to the club's mission.

Positions Open

  • Sub-Coordinator for 2K23 batch

Eligibility Criteria

  • Should not be a member of another technical club

Selection Process

  • Interview-based recruitment

Evaluation Criteria

  • Leadership and initiative
  • Communication skills
  • Technical knowledge relevant to the club's focus
  • Team collaboration
  • Past contributions and future vision

Code of Conduct

  • Must sign code of conduct and confidentiality agreement
  • Regular participation in meetings and events
  • Fulfill responsibilities as per the assigned role

Tenure

Sub-Coordinator: One academic year (renewable based on performance)

Removal/Resignation

  • Misconduct or violation of club policies
  • Non-performance
  • Voluntary resignation must be approved by the faculty mentor

Amendments

This rule book can be revised with :-

  • Majority approval by the core committee
  • Consent from the faculty mentor
